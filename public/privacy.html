Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

At STYLD, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

Profile data: Information you optionally provide in your style profile (gender, fit preference, budget, favorite brands, climate). This is stored to personalize your AI analysis.

Uploaded content: Photos and videos you upload for outfit analysis. These are sent to Anthropic's API for AI processing and are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of your session.

Fit history: Thumbnails and analysis results from past fits you've submitted, stored so you can revisit them in-app.

Usage data: Anonymized analytics (page views, feature usage) via Vercel Analytics to help us improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the AI styling service
  • To save your fit history and style profile across sessions
  • To authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
  • To improve and develop new features
  • To communicate product updates (only if you opt in)

3. AI Processing

Images you upload are sent to Anthropic (the makers of Claude) for AI analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle this data. We do not use your images to train AI models, and Anthropic's API terms prohibit use of API inputs for training without consent.

Learn more at anthropic.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

Your account and profile data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third-party services necessary to operate STYLD:

  • Google — for authentication via OAuth
  • Anthropic — for AI-powered outfit analysis
  • Supabase — for database and authentication infrastructure
  • Vercel — for hosting and anonymized analytics

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any other parties.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

STYLD uses browser local storage to maintain your session and remember preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Update or correct your profile information at any time in-app
  • Withdraw consent for data processing by deleting your account

To request account deletion or a data export, email trevor@saintstu.com.

8. Children's Privacy

STYLD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service evolves. We'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you via email or in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at trevor@saintstu.com.
